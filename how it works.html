How It Works

For Freelancers

  1. Sign Up: Click on the "Sign Up" button and fill out the registration form with your basic information.
  2. Create Your Portfolio: Fill out our detailed form to provide information about your skills, experience, and projects. Add links to your social media profiles like Facebook and Telegram.
  3. Share Your Portfolio: After completing your portfolio, you'll receive a unique link that you can share with potential clients.

For Clients

  1. Browse and Search: Browse our platform or use the search function to find freelancers by skills, experience, or other criteria.
  2. Discover Portfolios: Click on any freelancer's profile to view their portfolio and contact options.
  3. Contact Freelancers: Contact freelancers directly via their listed social media profiles, such as Facebook or Telegram.
